Welcome, Communists.

We are the Clonturk Communist Party. We are a political party of third years focused on bringing Communism to Clonturk. We believe that every student should become a Communist and we hope that we can bring change to this school through compassion, ambition, and of course, propaganda. If this sounds like something you might be interested in, see the Get Involved page to learn more.

The Clonturk Communist Party was founded on the 28th of February 2024, when Ashley had an idea to bring Communism to Clonturk. Since then, it has grown to include over like a few members, striving to bring Communism to the school we all know and love. One of our secondary missions is to spread awareness about Communism, what it means, and how you can make a difference. If you are curious about our Student Council Election Campaign, visit our About Page.

We are a student group independent of the school. We are not affiliated with the school, and our group is not managed or run by the school staff.

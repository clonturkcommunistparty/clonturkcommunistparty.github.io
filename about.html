Founding

The Clonturk Communist Party was founded on the 28th of February by Joshua Halpin. It quickly expanded to include more than half a dozen members and launched its Student Council Election Campaign. The purpose of this website is to inform people about our party and advertise our campaign. Our founder Joshua Halpin will be running for 3rd year student council in the Winter of 2024. If you wish to run for your year, you can contact us and we will help you in any way you may need.

Ethos

Our party believes that the effort you put into your work and life should be rewarded. We wish to implement a socialist system in Clonturk.

We are a student group independent of the school. We are not affiliated with the school, and our group is not managed or run by the school staff.

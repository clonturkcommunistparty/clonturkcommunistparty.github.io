Founding

The Clonturk Communist Party was founded on the 28th of February by Ashley. It quickly expanded to include more than about 3ish members and launched its Student Council Election Campaign. The purpose of this website is to inform people about our party and advertise our campaign. Our founder Ashley will be running for 3rd year student council in the Winter of 2024. If you wish to run for your year, you can contact us and we will help you in any way you may need.

Main Members

We are a student group independent of the school. We are not affiliated with the school, and our group is not managed or run by the school staff.
